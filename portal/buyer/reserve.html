New Home Reservation Agreement

By signing this reservation agreement, you agree to reserve the property at for the purchase price shown. The reservation fee is non-refundable except in the following circumstances:

  • The developer withdraws the property from sale
  • Unsatisfactory survey results (full structural survey)
  • Mortgage application rejection (with Decision in Principle evidence)
  • Material defects discovered during conveyancing
  • Adverse environmental or legal searches

The reservation period is 28 days from the date of signing, during which time you must instruct a solicitor and work towards exchange of contracts. The developer will remove the property from the market during this period.

Your Obligations:

  • Provide proof of funds within 7 days
  • Instruct a solicitor within 7 days
  • Respond to developer and solicitor communications within 48 hours
  • Work diligently towards exchange of contracts

All buyers named in this agreement must sign this document. The reservation will be valid once all signatures are collected.
