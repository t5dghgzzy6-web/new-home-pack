How To: Upload a Document

Upload a Document to a Plot

  1. Go to the Plots section.
  2. Select the plot you want to upload a document for.
  3. Click Upload Document (or the relevant button).
  4. Choose your file and add any notes or details.
  5. Click Save to upload the document to that plot.

Upload a Document to a Development

  1. Go to the Developments section.
  2. Select the development you want to upload a document for.
  3. Click Upload Document (or the relevant button).
  4. Choose your file and add any notes or details.
  5. Click Save to upload the document to that development.
